Our Gelateria

We are a family owned and operated Italian ice cream (gelato) stand serving neighborhood farmers markets in the Northern Virginia area. Conecopia Gelato uses the same methods as Italian artisans to produce "scratch made" small batch gelato using only fresh, high-quality ingredients. We take pride in creating seasonal flavors using the time-honored traditions of Italy. So, stop by and enjoy our fresh artisan gelato made locally for you.
